About Us

Pokelytro is a modern Pokédex to discover Pokémon, explore types, and build teams. The idea is simple: a fast, clean, and beautiful experience.

Angular 21 TypeScript Express MongoDB
Our Mission

Make discovering Pokémon fast, clear, and enjoyable: simple search, clean screens, and a consistent experience.

What We Build

A Pokédex with useful details (types, stats, evolutions) and a team builder designed for playing and learning.

The Experience

Polished details and seamless improvements designed to make the app feel robust, fast, and "pro".

What you’ll find in Pokelytro

  • Fast navigation across all Pokemon
  • Polished and intuitive interface
  • Complete Pokemon search tool
  • Team Builder to save and manage your teams
  • Type effectiveness chart for strategy
